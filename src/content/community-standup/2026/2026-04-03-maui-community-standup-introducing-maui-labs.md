There's been a wave of experimental work happening around .NET MAUI — new platform targets, alternative rendering approaches, and community-driven projects. This Community Standup introduces **maui-labs**, an official GitHub home that brings all of that work together in one place.

David Ortinau and Gerald Versluis walk through the repository and what it means for developers who want to explore what's next for .NET MAUI.
