This Community Standup dives into one of the biggest runtime changes coming to .NET MAUI: the move from Mono to CoreCLR in .NET 11. Šimon Rozsíval walks through what this means in practice — from startup time improvements to new configuration options — and demos the profiling tools he's been adding to MAUI Labs.

David Ortinau and Gerald Versluis host as Šimon covers community links, the CoreCLR transition, configuration comparisons (startup time vs APK size), custom app profiles, and a live demo of profiling a .NET MAUI app with the new tooling.
