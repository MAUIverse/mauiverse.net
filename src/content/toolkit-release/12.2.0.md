Some more fixes related to Popup and then some... Also, this release reinstates the assembly versioning that got lost in translation somewhere. Most people probably didn't even notice or care. But if you're one of the people who does... Now its fixed!

## What's Changed

* Adds installation instructions to README by [Vladislav Antonyuk](/community-contributors/VladislavAntonyuk/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2797
* Fire `Action PopupOptions.OnTappingOutsideOfPopup` when Android Back Button Pressed by [Brandon Minnick](/community-contributors/TheCodeTraveler/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2799
* Prevent Popup From Being Dismissed when Tapped Inside Popup by [Shaun Lawrence](/community-contributors/bijington/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2800
* Reinstate assembly versioning by [Gerald Versluis](/community-contributors/jfversluis/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2837
* Refactor TextCaseConverter and enhance ListToStringConverterTests by [@waheez](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=waheez) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2840
* Fix typo in the example label text on IsInRangeConverterPage by [@ruyut](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=ruyut) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2846
