Wowowow! So much community love in this release! [@GeorgeLeithead](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=GeorgeLeithead) brought back the `AvatarView` from the Xamarin Community Toolkit, now available for the .NET MAUI Community Toolkit. And Ryan introduced a completely new concept: you can now easily register view and viewmodel pairs in your dependency injection container!

These were both huge efforts and our team and them have been working closely together to make it happen and make sure we got things merged in a timely manner. Thanks everyone for this amazing work! 

## What's Changed
* IServiceCollectionExtensions by [@rjygraham](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=rjygraham) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/494
* Make sure the string `UseMauiCommunityToolkit` isn't a comment by [Pedro Jesus](/community-contributors/pictos/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/504
* Fix #500 Color is Null. Fix KeyWindow not correctly calculated by [Vladislav Antonyuk](/community-contributors/VladislavAntonyuk/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/502
* 104 avatar view by [@GeorgeLeithead](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=GeorgeLeithead) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/481
