## `CommunityToolkit.Maui v14.2.0`

Thanks to [@IeuanWalker](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=IeuanWalker), Android Screen Reader now properly reads Popup v2! Previously the screen reader would default to the underlying `PopupPage`, now it defaults directly to the content of your Popup:
