Just before the weekend, here are some new goodies to play with!

Minor new functionalities, bugfixes and we updated the dependency of .NET MAUI to 8.0.71 to match the latest version that is included in the workload that comes with Visual Studio.

## What's Changed

* Bump xunit.runner.visualstudio from 2.8.1 to 2.8.2 in /samples by @dependabot in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2010
* Bump xunit from 2.8.1 to 2.9.0 in /samples by @dependabot in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2009
* [Housekeeping] Update CI Pipeline to use macOS Sonoma and Xcode 15.3.0 by [@brminnick](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=brminnick) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/1969
* Information label and bound value in CompareConverter sample don't match by [@GeorgeLeithead](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=GeorgeLeithead) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/1981
* Update IconTintColorBehavior so it applies tint to Button image on Android and Windows like iOS and Mac Catalyst by [@mikeparker104](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=mikeparker104) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2042
* Remove `Microsoft.Maui.Controls.Compatibility` Dependency  by [@filipnavara](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=filipnavara) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2006
* [Sample App] Add `<UseMaui>true</UseMaui>` by [James Crutchley](/community-contributors/ne0rrmatrix/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2057
* Fix Target Type Validation by [@brminnick](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=brminnick) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2034
* fix: Fix unhandled ObjectDisposedException in IconTintColorBehavior.ClearTintColor on Android by [@Kay-STL](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=Kay-STL) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2012
* Transparent status bar color android by [Victor Hugo Garcia](/community-contributors/vhugogarcia/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/1630
* Source Generator for Bindable Property by [Pedro Jesus](/community-contributors/pictos/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/1321
* Update to Xcode 15.4 in CI pipelines by [@Kay-STL](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=Kay-STL) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2100
* Catch ObjectDisposedException in IconTintColorBehavior by [@Kay-STL](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=Kay-STL) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/2099
