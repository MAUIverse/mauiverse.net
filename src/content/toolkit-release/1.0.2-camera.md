All beginnings are hard... Despite our extensive testing it seems we still had a bug in our Android implementation that would crash your application when you captured an image. 

Luckily, our amazing [Shaun Lawrence](/community-contributors/bijington/) quickly found out why it happened and fixed it and because it's such a nasty one that will block you... Releasing it right away for you to enjoy! 🚀 

## What's Changed
* Remove ArrayPool usage for now to avoid BufferUnderflow by [Shaun Lawrence](/community-contributors/bijington/) in https://github.com/CommunityToolkit/Maui/pull/1961
