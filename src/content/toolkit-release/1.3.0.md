There is too much good stuff going on in this release! Thanks to [@GeorgeLeithead](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=GeorgeLeithead) we now have the `GravatarImageSource` also available in the .NET MAUI Community Toolkit. And [Shaun Lawrence](/community-contributors/bijington/) added a pre-built `FadeAnimation` that you can now use on all of your .NET MAUI elements.

Last but not least, our very own [Pedro Jesus](/community-contributors/pictos/), with big help from [@KSemenenko](/repo-contributors/community-toolkit/?q=KSemenenko), has implemented the Status Bar color functionality. With this you can easily change your color of the status bar at runtime! I know a lot of people were waiting for this one...

One thing to still mention, our newest core team member [Kym Phillpotts ](/community-contributors/kphillpotts/) has made his first contribution here too 🙌 

Also lots of bugfixes and other improvements, see the full list below. As always: big thanks to everyone that helped make this happen! 🥰
