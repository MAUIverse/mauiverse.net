The **June 2026** standup is packed with release news and architecture discussion, including what is now available and what is likely to change soon in Toolkit packages.

### What you'll learn

- `CommunityToolkit.Maui.Camera` **6.1.0** and the implementation of `IsTorchOn` in `CameraView`.
- `CommunityToolkit.Maui.MediaElement` streaming support with `MediaSource.FromStream`.
- Context and correction around the `14.2` release/tag workflow and what actually shipped.
- Accessibility and rendering fixes in popup and drawing scenarios.
- Maintainer discussion on deprecating `CommunityToolkit.Maui.Maps` and guiding developers to supported alternatives.
- Current popup concurrency edge cases, expected usage patterns, and investigation direction.

Watch this one to stay current on both the Toolkit APIs you can use today and the package-level decisions that may affect your upgrade plans.
