# .NET MAUI 

.NET MAUI is a [Current release](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/platform/support/policy/maui) and will be [supported by Microsoft](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/platform/support/policy/maui) for a minimum of 6 months after the next major release ships. Servicing releases will occur on the same monthly release cadence as .NET.

- [Known Issue](https://github.com/dotnet/maui/wiki/Known-Issues)
- [Installation and Getting Started](https://docs.microsoft.com/dotnet/maui/get-started/first-app?pivots=devices-android)
- [Documentation](https://docs.microsoft.com/dotnet/maui/)
