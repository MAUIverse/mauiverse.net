<p>In this episode of the Xamarin Podcast, Pierce Boggan and James Montemagno discuss all of the fun features from the new Visual Studio 2017 version 15.6 stable release, including macOS environment setup and iOS WiFi deployment. They also discuss building AR and ML into your mobile apps, and take a look at how you can add Tizen as a deployment target for your Xamarin.Forms apps.</p>

<p>3:30 HOT BITS! Visual Studio 2017 version 15.6<br>
10:30 Build some AR into your apps with UrhoSharp 1.8<br>
14:00 Navigation patterns in Xamarin.Forms<br>
19:00 Building apps for Tizen devices (phones, tablets, watches, and tvs!)<br>
22:00 Update your tvOS apps on the fly with tvML<br>
24:10 Machine learning with CoreML + Xamarin + Custom Vision Service<br>
28:10 Behaviors in Xamarin.Forms<br>
28:45 Adding a search bar to iOS lists<br>
30:30 BONUS Bachelor Finale thoughts!</p>
