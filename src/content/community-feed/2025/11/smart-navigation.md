Matt Goldman has renamed PageResolver to **SmartNavigation** and brought it up to date for **.NET 10**, cleaning up naming/namespaces and making the library’s purpose more discoverable.

The post walks through what changed (and what didn’t), including the unified `INavigationManager` API surface and some lifecycle improvements for ViewModels.
