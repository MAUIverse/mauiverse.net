This session pushes the project further from concept to repeatable architecture by tightening the data flow between device and backend. The focus is practical: identify weak spots, adjust design, and keep the system observable while features are added.

### What you will learn

- How to refine cross-service boundaries in a MAUI + Aspire + Orleans solution
- How GPS payload handling impacts downstream processing and responsiveness
- How to improve reliability while keeping the dev loop fast
- What to measure as the app transitions toward larger scale usage
