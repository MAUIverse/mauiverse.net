Support planning can get confusing when teams assume MAUI aligns one-to-one with broader .NET LTS messaging. Gerald breaks down the MAUI support model and explains why running on the latest major release is often the safest operational strategy.

### What you will learn

- How MAUI support cadence differs from general .NET LTS assumptions
- Why staying on the newest MAUI major version reduces support risk
- How to frame upgrade cadence as part of normal app maintenance

### Practical takeaway

- Treat MAUI version upgrades as a recurring engineering task, not a once-per-LTS-cycle event
