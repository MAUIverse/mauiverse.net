Syncfusion kicked off 2025 with a short preview of what’s coming in **Essential Studio 2025 Volume 1** for .NET MAUI.

The post highlights a new **Toolbar** control and improvements across core components like **Busy Indicator**, **DataGrid** (master-details + search), editor pickers (relative view + blackout dates), and **PDF Viewer** field customization/visibility.
