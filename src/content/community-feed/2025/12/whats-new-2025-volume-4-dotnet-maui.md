Syncfusion shared a look at **Essential Studio 2025 Volume 4** updates for .NET MAUI.

Highlights include new “smart” controls (Smart DataGrid, Smart Scheduler, Smart Text Editor), the **Liquid Glass** visual effect for iOS/MacCatalyst, and a broad set of enhancements across existing controls (DataGrid, charts, Scheduler, ListView, and more).
