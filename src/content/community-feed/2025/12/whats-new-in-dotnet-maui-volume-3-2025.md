Paul Anderson shared a preview of what’s coming in **Essential Studio 2025 Volume 3** for .NET MAUI.

The big headline features are new **Rich Text Editor** and **Markdown Viewer** controls, plus upgrades across core components like DataGrid (add-row + tooltips), Kanban, Scheduler (horizontal resource view), Sunburst Chart (selection + drill-down), and a more flexible Toolbar selection model.
