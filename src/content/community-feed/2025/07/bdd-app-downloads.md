Download progress UI can be functional without being boring. Matt Goldman explores a series of progressively better download-state patterns in a .NET MAUI app, starting with the classic blocking modal and moving toward richer per-item feedback.

It culminates in a delightfully over-the-top “beer glass fill” progress microinteraction built with SkiaSharp—complete with bubbles and a foam finale.
