Recorded live at Microsoft Build, this episode is part product discussion and part MAUI history lesson. Beth talks through how Xamarin evolved into .NET MAUI, where Blazor and web-based UI fit into native app development, and how the team balances compatibility, ecosystem growth, and developer productivity.

## What you'll learn

- How Beth thinks about Blazor WebView, Hybrid WebView, and choosing the right mix of web and native UI.
- Why MAUI’s evolution from Xamarin still shapes app architecture, tooling, and community expectations.
- How topics like AI-assisted development, open source, and cross-platform design are influencing the MAUI ecosystem right now.
