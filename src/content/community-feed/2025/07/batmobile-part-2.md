In part 2 of the Batmobile series, Matt Goldman builds the “Batcave” side: a live RPM dashboard rendered with .NET MAUI Graphics.

It breaks the gauge into drawable layers (background, redline zone, and pointer) and shows how to wire telemetry updates so the needle redraws in real time.
