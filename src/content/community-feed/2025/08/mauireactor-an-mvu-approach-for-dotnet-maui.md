If you like the “UI as a function of state” model (React/Flutter vibes) and want it in MAUI, **MauiReactor** is one of the most interesting projects in the ecosystem.

In this guest post, creator **Adolfo Marinucci** explains the MVU approach, why it can reduce boilerplate compared to classic MVVM, and how it enables a fast dev loop with strong C# tooling.
