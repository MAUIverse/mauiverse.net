This talk frames .NET MAUI in the context of **Xamarin.Forms**—what carries forward, what changes, and why the evolution matters.

If you’ve shipped Xamarin.Forms apps, it’s a useful perspective for understanding how MAUI’s model and tooling are meant to simplify the cross-platform story going forward.
