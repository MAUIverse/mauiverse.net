Gauges aren’t just for dashboards—they’re a great UI pattern any time you want “at a glance” status (health, energy, progress, etc.).

In this article, **Leomaris Reyes** explores the Telerik Gauge controls for .NET MAUI and the different styles you can use:

- **Radial** gauges (speedometer style)
- **Vertical** gauges
- **Horizontal** gauges
