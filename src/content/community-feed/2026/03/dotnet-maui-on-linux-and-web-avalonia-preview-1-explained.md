The first preview of Avalonia MAUI introduces a set of handlers that let you run .NET MAUI applications on platforms like Linux and WebAssembly — without modifying your existing app code. Javier, who has been directly involved in the development at Avalonia, walks through the preview in detail.

## What you'll learn

- What Avalonia MAUI Preview 1 is and why it matters for cross-platform reach
- A walkthrough of the gallery showcasing all available pages and controls
- How .NET MAUI apps look and behave when running on Linux
- The setup and configuration required to get started with the Avalonia backend
- Real app demos running on the new platform targets
- Current status, known limitations, and what's coming next
