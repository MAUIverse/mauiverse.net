KML Recorder is a .NET MAUI utility from the Shiny ecosystem that records GPS tracks and exports them as KML files — useful for mapping, geofencing testing, or any scenario where you need to capture and replay location data.

## What you'll find

- GPS track recording with KML export for .NET MAUI
- Built on the Shiny location and geospatial libraries
