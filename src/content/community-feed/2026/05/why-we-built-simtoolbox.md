Navigating simulator directories, digging through UUIDs, and querying databases by hand are tiny taxes iOS developers pay dozens of times a week. SimToolbox sits in your menu bar and eliminates that friction with instant access to every simulator on your machine.

## What you'll learn

- **Browse:** Jump straight to any app's Bundle, Documents, Caches, or Library directory without navigating UUIDs
- **Inspect:** Simulator Inspector for disk usage, Bundle Inspector for `Info.plist` and entitlements, Storage Inspector for visual disk usage breakdowns
- **Watch:** Database Watcher monitors SQLite in real time with row-level change detection, Filesystem Watcher streams file additions/modifications/deletions, User Defaults Watcher tracks preference changes
- Available on the Mac App Store for macOS development workflows
