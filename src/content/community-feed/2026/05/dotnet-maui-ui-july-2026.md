It's time for #MAUIUIJuly again! Based on an idea originally started for Xamarin by Steven Thewissen, MAUI UI July is a month-long, community-driven event where anyone gets to share their enthusiasm for building great .NET MAUI user interfaces.

## What you'll learn

- How to participate: pick a topic, choose a day in July, and create a blog post or video about .NET MAUI UI
- Content guidelines: English language (or English version), link back to the announcement post, and use the #dotnetmaui and #MAUIUIJuly hashtags
- Topic ideas: replicate well-known app UIs, build Dribbble designs with .NET MAUI, deep dive into UI features, or create custom controls
- The 2026 schedule with available dates and community members already signed up
- Links to previous years' contributions from 2022 through 2025 for inspiration
