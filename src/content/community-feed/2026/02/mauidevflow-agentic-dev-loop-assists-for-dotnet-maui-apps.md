MauiDevFlow is a tooling suite for automated MAUI development loops, including app inspection, interaction, logging, and diagnostics from the terminal.

The project includes CLI utilities, platform-aware drivers, and integrations aimed at improving iterative debugging for both humans and AI workflows.
