If your MAUI UI looks great on one device and falls apart on another, it’s usually not a “bug” — it’s a layout strategy issue.

This Syncfusion post is a strong practical refresher on responsive layout basics in .NET MAUI: avoid hard-coded sizes, lean on `Grid` and stack layouts, and treat spacing/padding as first-class design decisions.
