SkiaSharp 4.0 is a major upgrade to the cross-platform 2D graphics library that powers rendering across .NET MAUI, Blazor, and more. Under the hood, the Skia engine jumps 28 milestones with rendering quality, codec, and performance improvements that benefit every SkiaSharp app automatically.

## What you'll learn

- What's new in the Skia engine: mipmap sharpening, automatic Exif rotation, improved large image handling, and security hardening
- New capabilities: variable font axis control, color font palettes (OpenType CPAL), `SKPathBuilder` as the modern way to construct paths, and Linux Bionic/Tizen targets
- Uno Platform joining as co-maintainer: what this means for update cadence, stability, and community contribution
- The roadmap to SkiaSharp 4.0 GA this summer, including the new interactive Blazor WebAssembly gallery
- The live SkiaSharp event on June 30 hosted by Uno Platform with .NET team members
