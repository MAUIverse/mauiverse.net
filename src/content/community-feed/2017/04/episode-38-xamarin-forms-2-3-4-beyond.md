<p>In this episode of the Xamarin Podcast, Pierce Boggan is joined by David Ortinau, PM at Microsoft for Xamarin.Forms, to discuss the release of Xamarin.Forms 2.3.4 and what&#39;s on the roadmap for Xamarin.Forms moving forward.</p>

<p>Show Notes:</p>

<p>01:30 - Introducing the new PM for Xamarin.Forms, David Ortinau</p>

<p>02:30 - What does a typical day look like for David as a PM?</p>

<p>04:30 - Release of Xamarin.Forms 2.3.4 to stable</p>

<p>05:50 - Performance &amp; quality improvements</p>

<p>12:00 - Enhancements to XAMLC</p>

<p>13:50 - New bindable picker control</p>

<p>16:00 - Evolution forum for evolving the future of Xamarin.Forms</p>

<p>17:00 - OnIdiom support for desktop</p>

<p>22:25 - New &quot;nightly&quot; master builds for Xamarin.Forms and other fun ways of trying preview features</p>

<p>27:55 - What are fast renderers? How can they improve the performance of Xamarin.Forms apps?</p>
