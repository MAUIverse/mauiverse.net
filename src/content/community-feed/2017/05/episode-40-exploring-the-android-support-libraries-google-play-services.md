<p>In this episode of the Xamarin Podcast, James Montemagno is joined by Jon Dick of the Xamarin components team to discuss the Android Support Libraries and Google Play Services. Don&#39;t miss this super informative episode overviewing what these important libraries are, why they are necessary, what&#39;s new, and how to use them in your apps today.</p>

<p>7:00 - What are the Android Support Libraries?<br>
8:30 - Is it possible to build your app <em>without</em> the Android Support Libraries?<br>
11:45 - What does each Android Support Library do?<br>
15:00 - Versioning for Android Support Libraries, especially in relation to Xamarin.Forms<br>
16:45 - Should you always be using the latest version of the Android Support Libraries?<br>
25:00 - New things in the Android Support Libraries<br>
33:00 - Overview of Google Play Services<br>
44:00 - Tool/Package/Extension of the Week</p>
