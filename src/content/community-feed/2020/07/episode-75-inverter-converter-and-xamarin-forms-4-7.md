<p>Xamarin.Forms 4.7 is out and the Inverter Converter is all the rage!!</p>

<p>... well ... multi-value bindings are all the rage - but that doesn&#39;t have quite the ring of Inverter Converter.</p>

<p>This month James and Matt run down the rest of the great features in Xamarin.Forms 4.7 including how it helps you manage light and dark themes, eliminates the need for James&#39; image circle plug-in, and makes writing Grid row and column definitions so, so, so much easier.</p>

<p>Tune in to hear about some new requirements for submitting Android 10 apps to the Google Play store and get some quick tips on implementing back navigation with Xamarin.Forms Shell too!</p>
