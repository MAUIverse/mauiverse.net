<p>Lots of new stuff to talk about this month!</p>

<p>Ever want to get down to the nitty gritty and do some drawing in Xamarin.Forms! Well, now in Xamarin.Forms 4.8 you can!</p>

<p>James and Matt discuss all the ins and outs of brushes, gradients and shapes and how you can implement them in your Xamarin.Forms app.</p>

<p>There&#39;s also new releases for Visual Studio and Visual Studio for Mac. Find out about how you can make your .NET meetups shine - and attend some new ones too.</p>

<p>And tell us all about your experience about Entity Framework and Xamarin.</p>

<p>And of course, the Azure Service of the Month and Pick of the Pod!</p>
