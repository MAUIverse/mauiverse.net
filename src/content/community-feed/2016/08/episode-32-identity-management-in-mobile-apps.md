In this edition of the Xamarin Podcast, Pierce (@pierceboggan) and Mike (@mikecodesdotnet) discuss identity in mobile apps.  We start by asking: does my mobile app even need identity? If it does, we cover the different options available to developers, from local to social to enterprise authentication.

Special Guest: Mike James.
