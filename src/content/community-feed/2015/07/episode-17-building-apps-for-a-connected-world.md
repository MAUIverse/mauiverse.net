Building apps for a connected world is tough! Mobile devices, even in areas of high connectivity, are constantly gaining and losing internet connections. Sometimes, even in perfect scenarios things can go wrong. We also want our apps to be super-performant and work offline. In today's episode of the Xamarin Podcast, we break down our strategies for building fast, connected apps with Xamarin.

Huge shoutout to Rob Gibbens, who inspired this podcast with his excellent post on resilient network services: http://arteksoftware.com/resilient-network-services-with-xamarin/

Special Guest: Mike James.
