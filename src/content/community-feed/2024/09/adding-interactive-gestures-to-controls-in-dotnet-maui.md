Gestures are one of the easiest ways to make a MAUI app feel more native and responsive to user intent. James walks through common interaction patterns and keeps the focus on practical implementation rather than abstract theory.

## What you'll learn

- How gesture recognizers can be attached to MAUI controls in XAML and code.
- Where swipe interactions make sense for mobile-friendly experiences.
- Why small gesture-driven details can improve the feel of everyday app workflows.
