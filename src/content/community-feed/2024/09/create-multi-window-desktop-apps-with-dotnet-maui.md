James takes a desktop-first capability that is easy to overlook in MAUI and makes it concrete with a sample workflow. The session shows how multiple windows can expand what your app can do on desktop without losing sight of cross-platform behavior.

## What you'll learn

- How .NET MAUI supports multi-window app patterns on desktop platforms.
- What changes when you design for both desktop flexibility and mobile constraints.
- Why window management is worth understanding if your MAUI app spans more than phone-sized experiences.
