This episode blends community feedback with implementation talk, covering AI-assisted coding workflows, platform update pressure, and a hands-on .NET MAUI button scenario. It also connects these ideas to app maintainability, testing, and shipping mobile features across platforms.

## What you'll learn

- How to use ChatGPT and Copilot as coding assistants without treating them like fully autonomous tools.
- Why iOS/Xcode update cadence affects .NET MAUI delivery planning and release timing.
- What to watch for when building custom content button behavior in cross-platform UI.
