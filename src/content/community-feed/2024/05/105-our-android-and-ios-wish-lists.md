The conversation covers conference expectations, foldables, permission UX, notification behaviors, app store friction, and evolving API stability. It also links platform trends to MAUI plugin strategy and practical feature planning across ecosystems.

## What you'll learn

- How platform policy and API churn influence roadmap decisions for app teams.
- Why permission and notification UX differences require platform-aware product design.
- How plugin ecosystems can bridge platform gaps before framework-level support arrives.
