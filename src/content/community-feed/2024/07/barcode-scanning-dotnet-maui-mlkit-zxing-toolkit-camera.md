Barcode scanning can power inventory flows, checkouts, and “scan-to-look-up” experiences—and there are multiple ways to implement it in .NET MAUI.

Vladislav Antonyuk compares **ML Kit (Android)**, **Camera.MAUI.ZXing**, and a **CommunityToolkit camera + ZXing** approach, including what each option is best suited for.
