Custom controls are where MAUI apps start to feel truly “yours”—but they also come with design, handler, and platform nuances.

In this On .NET Live session, Pedro talks through what it means to build and integrate **custom controls in .NET MAUI**, including the patterns that keep them maintainable across platforms.
