Jon and Allan discuss C# language additions, workload version sets, title bar customization, handler lifecycle improvements, embedding MAUI in native apps, and CollectionView handler evolution. They also discuss Windows development loop improvements and the value of escape hatches in architecture decisions.

## What you'll learn

- How workload version sets improve repeatability and stability across build environments.
- What new MAUI platform capabilities change UI customization and handler management.
- Why escape-hatch design matters when adopting framework or third-party dependencies.
