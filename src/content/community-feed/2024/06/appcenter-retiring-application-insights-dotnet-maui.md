With App Center retiring, many .NET MAUI teams are reassessing their telemetry pipeline.

Daniel Hindrikes shares a practical walkthrough for moving to **Application Insights**, covering what to instrument, where the gotchas are, and how to keep visibility into crashes and app behavior after the transition.
