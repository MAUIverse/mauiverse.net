This interview-style episode covers the practical and strategic side of building with MAUI and Blazor together. Beth talks through the latest MAUI direction, including the Hybrid WebView work, and frames web-plus-native development as a flexible tool rather than a one-size-fits-all pattern.

## What you'll learn

- How Beth thinks about combining Blazor and .NET MAUI in real applications.
- Why Hybrid WebView expands the options for teams mixing web content and native UI.
- Where MAUI fits for developers who want flexibility across mobile, desktop, and web-oriented skills.
