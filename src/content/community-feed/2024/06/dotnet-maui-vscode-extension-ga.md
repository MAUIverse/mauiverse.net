The .NET MAUI extension for VS Code moved from preview to **Generally Available**, with notable improvements to the XAML editing experience and Hot Reload workflows.

If you’re developing MAUI apps primarily in VS Code—especially on macOS—this post is a good overview of what’s included and what to expect.
