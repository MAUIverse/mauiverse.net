They discuss AndroidX dependency sprawl, iOS framework integration friction, bytecode and interop challenges, and message-passing approaches for platform channels. The conversation points to tooling gaps and opportunities for smoother binding workflows.

## What you'll learn

- How to scope binding surface area to avoid unnecessary native dependency burden.
- How platform channels can bridge native and .NET code with explicit boundaries.
- Which tooling limitations still make bindings one of the harder mobile tasks.
