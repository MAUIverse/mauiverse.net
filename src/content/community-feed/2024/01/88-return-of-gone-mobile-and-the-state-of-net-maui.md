They cover MAUI framework progress, Blazor WebView strategy, runtime and handler evolution, profiling workflow, and compiled binding productivity improvements. The discussion encourages teams to measure early and use data to drive optimization choices.

## What you'll learn

- How MAUI's handler model and source-generation direction shape modern app design.
- How profiling with trace tools can reveal startup and runtime bottlenecks quickly.
- How compiled binding approaches reduce verbosity while improving XAML ergonomics.
