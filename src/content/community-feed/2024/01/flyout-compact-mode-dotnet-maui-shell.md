Shell makes navigation approachable, but desktop-style apps sometimes want a **compact flyout** rather than a full-width drawer.

In this video, Daniel Hindrikes shows how he configures a Flyout in compact mode with **.NET MAUI Shell**, including the key settings and layout decisions that make it feel right.
