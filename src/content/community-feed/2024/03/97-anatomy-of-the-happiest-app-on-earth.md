They analyze reservation flows, check-in, navigation, wallet integration, Bluetooth interactions, real-time photo experiences, and crowd-management systems behind a highly integrated app. The episode highlights how coordinated backend + device capabilities create perceived magic for users.

## What you'll learn

- How tightly integrated app journeys combine payments, location, and identity systems.
- How BLE and map experiences can improve contextual interactions in large venues.
- How operational analytics and backend orchestration shape frontline mobile UX.
