The conversation spans rendering model differences, WebView performance, service injection into Razor components, shared ViewModel strategies, and business implications of cross-platform talent reuse. It positions hybrid as a flexible option rather than a universal replacement.

## What you'll learn

- How Blazor Hybrid can share UI and logic while still accessing native APIs.
- Which platform/debug contexts most affect WebView performance experience.
- How to decide between MAUI native UI, Blazor Hybrid, and web-first approaches.
