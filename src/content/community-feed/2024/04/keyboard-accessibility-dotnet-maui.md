Keyboard accessibility is one of those areas that’s easy to miss until users are blocked by it.

Rachel walks through what “keyboard accessible” really means (including key WCAG criteria) and how MAUI’s design choices help: focus behavior on modal pages, avoiding keyboard traps, and adding keyboard accelerators for power users.
