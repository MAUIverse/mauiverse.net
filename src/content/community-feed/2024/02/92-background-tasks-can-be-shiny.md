Topics include transfers, scheduling, foreground/background service models, state passing, sync design, and battery-aware location tracking strategies. The episode keeps the guidance grounded in shipping constraints rather than idealized platform behavior.

## What you'll learn

- How platform-specific background policies shape architecture choices.
- How to manage state and retries safely across delayed or interrupted jobs.
- How to design background workflows that respect battery and data budgets.
