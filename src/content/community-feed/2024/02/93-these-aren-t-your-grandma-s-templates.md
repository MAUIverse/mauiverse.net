They discuss item templates, project/solution templates, VS and CLI differences, permission/entitlement defaults, and csproj-driven customization. The conversation emphasizes templates as a standardization tool for onboarding and repeatable architecture.

## What you'll learn

- How to choose between item, project, and solution template levels.
- How to bake platform permissions and build defaults into reusable templates.
- How template-driven standards reduce drift across multiple app codebases.
