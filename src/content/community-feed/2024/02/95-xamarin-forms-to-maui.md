They cover handlers, single-project structure, dependency migration, resource updates, image/font strategy, and stepwise porting techniques that reduce risk. The conversation highlights practical gotchas and replacement patterns teams hit during real upgrades.

## What you'll learn

- How to sequence migration work to keep builds stable while features move over.
- How handler-based customization differs from renderer-era extension points.
- How to modernize assets and resources with MAUI tooling during migration.
