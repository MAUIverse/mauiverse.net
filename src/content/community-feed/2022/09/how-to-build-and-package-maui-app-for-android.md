This session focuses on building and packaging a .NET MAUI app for Android and preparing it for deployment.

## What you'll learn

- How to generate Android-ready MAUI builds for testing or release.
- How packaging choices affect install behavior on real devices.
- How to validate the app after deployment.

Note: Signing and store-ready requirements for production releases are stricter than local test deployments.
