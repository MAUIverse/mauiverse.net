Badge counts are a small UX detail that can have a big impact for apps with notifications or “needs attention” workflows.

Vladislav Antonyuk outlines a cross-platform badge API for .NET MAUI and shows how each platform differs—especially Android launchers and required permissions—plus a Tizen implementation using `BadgeControl`.
