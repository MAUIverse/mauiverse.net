This talk is a fast and friendly walkthrough of MAUI basics, aimed at developers who want to understand the platform without getting buried in theory.

## What you'll learn

- The MAUI value proposition for building one app across multiple platforms.
- Key framework pieces you’ll interact with as you start building.
- Where to focus first when evaluating MAUI for your next app.

It’s a useful entry point if you want a high-signal overview before diving into implementation details.
