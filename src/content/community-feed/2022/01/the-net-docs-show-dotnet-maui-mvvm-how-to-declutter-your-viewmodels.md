This session focuses on a problem many MAUI developers hit once an app starts growing: ViewModels becoming too large, too busy, and too hard to maintain. Jesus brings the conversation back to cleaner MVVM structure and the habits that help keep responsibilities more organized.

## What you'll learn

- Why ViewModels become cluttered and how that impacts app maintainability.
- How to think about separating concerns in an MVVM-based MAUI app.
- Where small structural improvements can make state, commands, and view logic easier to reason about.
