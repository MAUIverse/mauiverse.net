This episode is designed as an entry point, so it spends time on what MAUI is, why it exists, and what kinds of native applications it helps you build. Jeff sets up the rest of the series by giving the framework enough context to make later topics easier to place.

## What you'll learn

- What .NET MAUI is and why it matters in the .NET ecosystem.
- How MAUI helps developers build native applications across multiple platforms.
- Why a strong conceptual introduction helps before you move into app architecture and UI detail.
