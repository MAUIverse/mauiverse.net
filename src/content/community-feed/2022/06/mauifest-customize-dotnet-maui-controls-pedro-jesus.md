Pedro’s MauiFest talk surveys the main strategies for customizing MAUI controls—what each approach buys you and when it’s the right tool.

It’s a solid overview if you’re moving from Xamarin.Forms concepts (effects/renderers) into MAUI-era patterns (handlers, mappers, platform behaviors, and more).
