This post is a hands-on walkthrough of MAUI’s `Map` control: adding pins, and drawing **polylines**, **polygons**, and **circles** to visualize areas and paths.

It’s especially useful if you’re building location-aware apps and want more than just markers—like route overlays, boundaries, and interactive map annotations.
