Shipping is part of the job. In part 1 of this series, Matt Goldman walks through the practical steps to publish a .NET MAUI app to the **Google Play Store**—from developer accounts and signing keys to test tracks and rollout.

It’s a detailed, “here’s what actually happens” guide aimed at reducing the friction (and surprises) of store submission.
