Consistent styling becomes much easier once you stop treating every control as a one-off. Maddy uses this episode to show how MAUI styling options fit together so you can build a cleaner visual system and reduce duplication in your XAML.

## What you'll learn

- The difference between explicit styles, implicit styles, and style classes.
- How reusable styling patterns help keep cross-platform UI more consistent.
- Why better styling structure pays off as an app grows.
