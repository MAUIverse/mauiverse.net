Borders are simple on the surface, but they influence the entire feel of a screen once you start refining card layouts, inputs, and containers. Maddy walks through the built-in border tooling and then goes a step further with more customized visual treatments.

## What you'll learn

- How to use the MAUI `Border` control to shape corners and edges.
- Where custom border treatments, including dashed lines, can help a design stand out.
- Why small framing details can make a UI look more deliberate and cohesive.
